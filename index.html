About GradCompass

GradCompass is an online consultancy platform for graduate admissions abroad. It was founded in 2024 by a group of students at NUST School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences (SEECS) led by Muhammad Ahmed Mohsin. Our mission is to provide students with the best possible guidance and resources to help them navigate their way through the complex process of applying to graduate programs abroad. We provide a range of services including profile evaluation, research matching with faculty, application assistance, and interview preparation. Our team of experienced mentors and consultants are here to help you every step of the way.

Our Services

Finding research domain and relevant faculty

STEP - 1

Spamming Emails

Our mentors will help you identify your research interests and find the best faculty members to work with. We will guide you through the process of identifying potential research domains and matching them with faculty members who share your interests. Moreoevr, we will help you reach out to faculty members and establish contact with them to discuss your research ideas.

Writing SoP

STEP - 2

Iterate through your SoP

In this step, we will help you craft a compelling Statement of Purpose (SoP) that highlights your academic achievements, research interests, and career goals. We will work with you to create a personalized SoP that showcases your unique strengths and experiences. Our mentors will provide feedback and suggestions to help you refine your SoP and make it stand out to admissions committees.

Improving CV

STEP - 3

Refining your CV according to academia standards

In this step, we will help you create a professional Curriculum Vitae (CV) that highlights your academic achievements, research experience, and relevant skills. We will work with you to format your CV according to academic standards and ensure that it effectively communicates your qualifications to admissions committees. Our mentors will provide feedback and suggestions to help you improve your CV and make it more competitive.

Writing diversity Statement

STEP - 4

Writing diversity and inclusion statement for some specific universities

In this step, we will help you write a compelling Diversity Statement that highlights your unique background, experiences, and perspectives. We will work with you to craft a personalized statement that showcases your commitment to diversity and inclusion. Our mentors will provide feedback and suggestions to help you refine your statement and make it resonate with admissions committees.
